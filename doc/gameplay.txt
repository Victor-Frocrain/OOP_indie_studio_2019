Bomberman is a very famous game, where all player have the same goal, they have to be the last player.

Gameplay involves strategically placing down bombs, which explode in multiple directions after a certain amount of time,
in order to destroy obstacles and kill other players.

The player can pick up various power-ups:
-BOMB : maximal bombs down at a time + 1 (start at 1)
-FIRE : bombs range + 1 (start at 1)
-SPEED : incresse the player speed
-WALLPASS : the player can pass inside breakable wall

The player is killed if they get caught up in a bomb's explosion, including their own,
requiring players to be cautious of their own bomb placement.
